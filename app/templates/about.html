our story

A small studio making paper goods with a whole lot of love.

Papier Lab is a tiny Filipino studio that handcrafts bookmarks, keychains, polaroid prints and crafts. We believe small things — a soft pink bow, a hand-written note — can make ordinary days a little cuter.

Made for readers, dreamers & gift-givers.

Every order is made-to-order in our tiny home studio. We source pastel paper, acrylic charms, and matte film, then hand-cut, laminate, and finish each piece with a bow before packing.

If you have an idea, just message us in chat. We absolutely love custom orders — names, dates, favourite quotes, polaroid sets of your loved ones, you name it.
