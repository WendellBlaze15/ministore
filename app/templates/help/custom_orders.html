What we can customise

  • Bookmarks with names, initials, song lyrics or favourite quotes.
  • Keychains with photo prints, miniature paintings, or holographic charms.
  • Polaroid sets of your loved ones, friend group or favourite trips.
  • Bundle gift boxes for birthdays, valentines and graduations.

How to place a custom order

  1. Open chat with the studio and tell us what you have in mind. Share inspiration photos if you'd like!
  2. We'll send a quick draft, confirm the price, and tell you the lead time.
  3. Pay via GCash or COD using our normal checkout.
  4. We craft your piece by hand and ship it to you in a cute kraft box. 🌸

Lead time

Custom orders typically take 3–7 working days of crafting before they ship. Bulk orders (e.g., wedding favours) need 2–3 weeks — please reach out early!

Pricing

Custom pieces start at the same price as our regular products. Add-ons like holographic finishes, gold foil, or premium charms have small surcharges that we'll quote upfront.
