How long until my order ships?

Every piece is handmade so please allow 2–4 working days of crafting before dispatch. Custom orders take 3–7 working days.

Do you ship outside the Philippines?

Not yet, sorry! We're a tiny home studio and we want to make sure every parcel arrives in perfect condition. International shipping is coming soon.

How do I pay?

You can pay through GCash or Cash on Delivery (COD). Both are available at checkout.

Can I cancel or change my order?

You can change your order while it's still in Pending status. Once we've started crafting, changes may not be possible. Reach out in chat as early as you can.

Do you take rush orders?

Sometimes! Message us first and we'll check our schedule. A small rush fee may apply.

Are your bookmarks laminated?

Yes — all bookmarks are sealed in glossy or matte film for extra durability. Polaroid prints use matte film.

Can I return an item?

Because most of our pieces are personalised, we don't accept returns. But if your parcel arrives damaged, please send us a photo within 3 days and we'll remake it for free.

How do I track my order?

Sign in and visit your orders page — every order gets its own tracking timeline.
