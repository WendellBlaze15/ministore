What we collect

When you place an order or sign up, we collect your name, email, phone number and delivery address. We also store your account password as a one-way hash — we never see the plain text.

Why we collect it

  • To process and ship your handmade orders.
  • To chat with you about your order or custom request.
  • To send a delivery update if something changes.

Who we share it with

Only the couriers (J&T, LBC, Flash Express, etc.) so they can deliver your parcel. We never sell your data and we never share it with marketers.

Cookies & sessions

We use a single session cookie to keep you signed in. It's marked HttpOnly and SameSite=Lax, so it's protected from common attacks.

Delete your data

You can delete your whole account anytime from your settings page. We'll wipe your profile, addresses, cart and chats.

Questions?

Just message us in chat or email papierlab8@gmail.com.
