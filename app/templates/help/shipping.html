Where we ship

We currently ship nationwide within the Philippines through trusted couriers like J&T, LBC and Flash Express. Metro Manila and Cebu deliveries usually arrive in 2–4 working days, while provincial areas take 4–7 working days.

Lead time

Because everything is made-to-order, please allow 2–4 working days of crafting time before your order is dispatched. Rush orders are sometimes available — message us in chat first.

Shipping fees

  • Metro Manila: ₱75–₱120
  • Luzon (outside MM): ₱150–₱200
  • Visayas: ₱180–₱220
  • Mindanao: ₱200–₱260

Exact fees are computed at checkout. We always pack your goodies in a cute kraft box with a bow and a thank-you note. 🌸

Tracking your order

Once your parcel ships, you'll see the courier and tracking number on your orders page. You can also chat the admin anytime to follow up — we love updates!

Lost or damaged parcels

If your parcel arrives damaged or never arrives, please message us within 3 days of the expected delivery date and we'll work with the courier or remake your order at no charge.
