our story

A pink desk, a craft knife, and lots of bows.

Papier Lab started in a tiny bedroom corner with a craft knife, pastel paper, and a dream of making study days a little cuter. What began as gifts for friends slowly turned into a small studio sending tiny pieces of joy to readers, journalers, and gift-givers everywhere.

Today we still hand-cut, hand-pack and hand-bow every order. We'd love for you to feel that warmth when your parcel arrives.
